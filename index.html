The Inspiration for the Song

The story goes that Cohn's was having a dry spell coming up with his signature music and lyrics. He was inspired by masterful singer and songwritter James Taylor who had said that he broke his writer's block by going somewhere he'd never been before and then got inspiration from that experience. Memphis was just the place for Cohn's.

As you learn from that song, Cohn did touristy things like visited Graceland but also asked friends for more obsure, off-the-beaten-path suggestions, which resulted in a visit to the Full Gospel Tabernacle church and the Hollywood Cafe'in Robinsonville, Mississippi.
